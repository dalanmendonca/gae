Datetime format

2000 - event that happened in that year
01/2000 - event that happened in that month/year
01/01/2000 - event that happened exactly on that day/month/year
2001-2005, 10/2001-02/03/2005 - event that happen between the two dates
~2005 - event that happened at around that year
2005-~ - event that happened from that year and beyond (now).
